# Customer_Behavior_Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes customer shopping behavior for a retail company to understand purchasing patterns, customer segments, and factors influencing sales.

The goal is to generate actionable insights that can help improve customer engagement, optimize marketing strategies, and increase overall revenue.

---

## Problem Statement
A retail company aims to better understand customer shopping behavior to improve sales, customer satisfaction, and long-term loyalty.

The company has observed variations in purchasing patterns across customer demographics, product categories, and sales channels. Key factors such as discounts, reviews, seasons, and payment methods are believed to influence customer decisions and repeat purchases.

---

## Dataset
The dataset contains customer-level transaction data, including:

- Customer ID  
- Age Group  
- Gender  
- Category & Product Purchased  
- Purchase Amount  
- Subscription Status  
- Discount Applied  
- Review Rating  
- Shipping Type  
- Previous Purchases  

---

## Tools Used
- SQL (PostgreSQL)  
- Python (Pandas)  
- Power BI (Dashboard)  

---

## Analysis Steps
1. Data understanding and exploration  
2. Data cleaning and preparation  
3. SQL analysis using:
   - Aggregations (SUM, AVG)
   - GROUP BY
   - Subqueries
   - CTEs
   - Window functions  
4. Customer segmentation analysis  
5. Revenue and product performance analysis  
6. Visualization using Power BI  

---

## Dashboard
The dashboard provides insights into:

- Total customers, average purchase amount, and ratings  
- Revenue and sales by category  
- Customer distribution by subscription status  
- Sales and revenue by age group  
- Customer segmentation filters (gender, category, shipping type)

## Key Results & Insights
- Clothing category generates the highest revenue among all categories  
- Majority of customers are non-subscribers, indicating growth potential  
- Young adults contribute the highest revenue segment  
- Discounts positively influence purchasing behavior  
- Repeat customers show higher engagement and spending
